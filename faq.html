Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

▼ How does LuxDraw work?
LuxDraw is a platform where you can buy tickets for a chance to win high-value items at a fraction of their retail price. Simply select a product, choose how many tickets you want, and wait for the draw. When all tickets for a product are sold, we conduct a live, transparent draw to determine the winner.
▼ How much are tickets?
Ticket prices vary depending on the product and its value. For example, you might find tickets for $2 up to $15, depending on what's being raffled. The price is clearly displayed on each product card.
▼ Can I buy multiple tickets?
Yes! You can purchase as many tickets as you want for each draw. More tickets increase your chances of winning. Each ticket is a separate entry into the draw.

Draws & Winning

▼ When do draws happen?
Draws happen automatically once all tickets for a product are sold. You'll receive an email notification when a draw you participated in is about to happen, and another email confirming the results.
▼ How is the winner selected?
Winners are selected using a certified random algorithm that ensures complete fairness. The draw process is transparent and independently verified. Results are published immediately after the draw.
▼ How do I know if I've won?
We'll send you an email immediately after the draw with the results. If you're the winner, you'll receive detailed information about claiming your prize and shipping details.
▼ What happens if I don't win?
Your tickets are specific to each draw. You can enter other active draws with new tickets, or wait for future draws of products you're interested in.

Payment & Safety

▼ Is my payment information safe?
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and work with trusted payment processors (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay). We never store your complete payment details on our servers.
▼ What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. You can select your preferred payment method during checkout.
▼ Can I get a refund?
Ticket purchases are generally non-refundable as they represent your entry into a draw. However, if you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@luxdraw.github.io.

Legal & Compliance

▼ Is LuxDraw legal?
Yes. LuxDraw operates as a legitimate raffle/draw service with transparent, certified practices. All draws are conducted fairly and in compliance with applicable regulations.
▼ What are the age requirements?
You must be at least 18 years old to participate in LuxDraw draws. We verify age during the registration and payment process.
▼ What is your privacy policy?
We take your privacy seriously. Read our complete Privacy Policy to understand how we collect and use your information.
